# Appendix J: Grey Labyrinth Shadow Plort Door Locations

All Shadow Plort-locked doors and collectors in the Grey Labyrinth endgame area.

**Total:** 25 Shadow Plort Doors / Collectors throughout the Grey Labyrinth.

**Total Shadow Plorts needed:** ~825 to open all of them.

> **Cost update (verified against MapGenie):** Patch 1.0.3 reduced Shadow Plort costs - a change the guide previously listed as *unverified*. The costs below now reflect the current (reduced) values; the earlier figures (and the old **1,165** total) were pre-patch. Current costs run **15 / 25 / 60 / 75** per door (no more 50s or 100s). MapGenie also distinguishes **Shadow Plort Collectors** (deposit plorts over time) from instant **Shadow Plort Doors**; both are listed together here. Two entries are marked `*` - **Wall Lighting** (not listed on MapGenie) and **Ancient Stabilizer** (matched to MapGenie's "Stabilizing Gate") - so the total is approximate; verify the last few in-game.
