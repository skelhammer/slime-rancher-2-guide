[h3]The Operational Blueprint[/h3]

This chapter has two jobs: crack all 25 Shadow Plort Doors and bank the resources for Prismacore stabilization. Grind now so the finale runs clean.

[hr][/hr]

[h3]Phase 1: Shadow Plort Door Completion[/h3]

[b]Objective:[/b] Open all 25 Shadow Plort Doors (~825 Shadow Plorts total)

[b]Shadow Plort Farming Strategy:[/b]
[list]
[*]Shadow Slimes spawn in groups of 2-5 in dark corners throughout Grey Labyrinth
[*]Hit them with ANY thrown object (Pink Plorts, vegetables, anything in the tank)
[*]They drop Shadow Plorts, then vanish into the dark
[*]They respawn in the same corner locations, so memorize your route
[*]Total needed: ~845 Shadow Plorts (~825 for doors, 20 for the Nullifier)
[*][b]⚠️ CRITICAL:[/b] Store these in SILOS, not the Refinery. Refinery plorts cannot open doors.
[/list]

[b]Door Cost Breakdown:[/b]
[list]
[*]Costs range from 15-75 Shadow Plorts per door (reduced in Patch 1.0.3)
[*]Total for all 25 doors: ~825 Shadow Plorts
[*]Priority Doors (open first): the 25 and 50 plort doors for shortcuts and quality-of-life
[/list]

[hr][/hr]

[h3]Phase 2: Prisma Plort Stockpiling[/h3]

[b]Objective:[/b] Bank ~245 Prisma Plorts total

[b]Prisma Plort Uses:[/b]
[list]
[*]90 for Gigi's milestone trades (Chapter 14)
[*]155 for crafting upgrades (145 Vacpack + 10 Disruption Detector)
[/list]

[b]Prisma Plort Farming Strategy:[/b]
[list]
[*]During Prisma Disruptions, all food turns "Unstable"
[*]Feed Unstable food to any slimes to get Unstable Plorts
[*]Walk through the Ancient Stabilizer and Unstable Plorts convert to Prisma Plorts in your inventory
[*]Conversion rate: 1 Unstable to 1-2 Prisma Plorts (usually 2)
[*][b]⚠️ CRITICAL:[/b] Store these in SILOS, not the Refinery. Gigi will not trade for Refinery plorts.
[/list]

[b]Optimal Farming Setup:[/b]
[list]
[*]Run a temporary Pink Slime operation near the Ancient Stabilizer in Grey Labyrinth
[*]Collect Unstable food during the disruption
[*]Feed the Pink Slimes for Unstable Plorts
[*]Walk through the Ancient Stabilizer to convert them to Prisma Plorts
[*]Deposit to a portable Refinery Link or haul it back to the ranch
[*]Keep clear of ground cracks during disruptions. Plasma and flame geysers do not negotiate.
[/list]

[h3]Phase 4: Craft the Disruption Detector[/h3]

[b]Blueprint:[/b] Obtained from Gigi in the Impossible Sky area

[b]Recipe:[/b] 500 NB + 10 Prisma Plorts + 5 Tin Petal + 5 Dream Bubble

[b]Usage:[/b] Place it in Grey Labyrinth to add map pins showing disruption status:
[list]
[*]White icon = no disruption
[*]Yellow icon = disruption incoming
[*]Red icon = disruption ongoing
[/list]

This turns Prisma farming from guesswork into a schedule. Cheap intel for an expensive grind.
