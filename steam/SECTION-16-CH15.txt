[h3]Phase 1: The Master Economist - Infinite Wealth[/h3]

[b]Advanced Market Manipulation:[/b]

[b]Objective:[/b] Generate massive single-day profit by artificially creating scarcity and demand

[b]Strategy:[/b]
[olist]
[*][b]Select a Target Plort:[/b] Choose high-value plort (Saber, Ringtail, or Hyper)
[*][b]Hoard:[/b] Dedicate several ranch expansions to producing only this plort. DO NOT SELL. Stockpile 1,000+ in Silos
[*][b]Crash the Market:[/b] Sell every other plort type daily. Flood market to drive prices down
[*][b]Wait for the Spike:[/b] Market algorithm will eventually create "high demand" day for hoarded plort (200%+ spike)
[*][b]Execute the Sell-Off:[/b] On spike day, sell entire stockpile for hundreds of thousands of Newbucks
[/olist]

[b]Engaging the Endgame Economy: ProntoMart & The Night Market:[/b]

[list]
[*][b]ProntoMart:[/b] Standard shop with Coo-Coo Corner (random daily items) and Polestar Provisions (permanent blueprints)
[*][b]The Night Market:[/b] Secret endgame tier
  - [b]Unlock:[/b] After stabilizing Prismacore and speaking with Gigi
  - [b]Availability:[/b] Only open during night (20:00 to 4:00 game time)
  - [b]Currency:[/b] Rainbow Coins (obtained by trading Prisma Plorts to Gigi)
  - [b]Wares:[/b] Exclusive items, prismatic color variants, advanced drone modules, unique decorations, final Sureshot upgrades
[/list]
