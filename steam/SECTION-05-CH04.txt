[h1]Part 1: The Foundation[/h1]

[h2]Chapter 4: Starlight Strand Asset Acquisition[/h2]

[b]Primary Objective:[/b] Finish exploring Starlight Strand and bank the resources that fund your Tier II upgrades.

[b]Mindset:[/b] This is a harvest run. The Conservatory is already printing money, so your job here is to pop every Gordo, grab every node, and walk out with the raw materials that turn a profitable ranch into an unstoppable one. One resource gates everything: Moondew Nectar. Get it first or lose the night.

[list]
[*][b]Starting Budget:[/b] 10,000-15,000+ NB carried over from Chapter 3
[*][b]Capital Required:[/b] ~11,100 NB total
  - Tier II upgrades: 7,850 NB
  - Warp infrastructure: 3,250 NB
[*][b]Daily Revenue:[/b] ~2,500+ NB from Conservatory operations
[*][b]Timeline:[/b] 3-4 days while the corrals keep earning
[/list]

[h3]Phase 1: Gordo Strategy[/h3]

Five Gordos, five payouts. Feed them and collect.

[b]Pop All 5 Starlight Strand Gordos:[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Hunter Gordo[/b] (50 Meat) = Azure Bushes + slime doorway shortcut + 12 Hunter Slimes
[*][b]Honey Gordo[/b] (50 Fruit) = Tank Liner + Phosphor plort shortcuts + 12 Honey Slimes
[*][b]Angler Gordo[/b] (50 Meat) = up-spout fast return route + 12 Angler Slimes
[*][b]Ringtail Gordo[/b] (50 Any, NIGHT ONLY) = portal to Rainbow Fields + 12 Ringtail Slimes
[*][b]Flutter Gordo[/b] (15 Moondew Nectar ONLY) = Research Drone + Teleporter + Grey Labyrinth gate + 12 Flutter Slimes
[/list]

[h3]Phase 4: Critical Resources[/h3]

[b]Harvest These Materials:[/b]
[list]
[*]50+ Buzz Wax (common throughout)
[*]50+ Wild Honey (hives on trees and cliffs)
[*]30+ Moondew Nectar (NIGHT ONLY, 5PM-6AM): [b]CRITICAL[/b]
[*]20-30 Sea Hens (coastal areas, Angler favorite)
[*]20-30 Mint Mangos (pink biome, Honey favorite)
[/list]

[b]⚠️ CRITICAL:[/b] Moondew Nectar only blooms at night (5 PM to 6 AM) in the glowing blue flowers and despawns at dawn. Grab 30+ the moment you can. Miss the window and you wait a full day.

[hr][/hr]

[h3]Phase 5: Warp Infrastructure[/h3]

[b]Deploy in Starlight Strand:[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Refinery Link[/b] (250 NB + materials): junction between the pink and blue biomes
[*][b]Starlight Strand Portal[/b] (2,000 NB + materials): one-way to Starlight Strand
[*][b]Green Home Portal[/b] (1,000 NB + materials): one-way to the Conservatory
[/list]

[b]Strategic Rationale:[/b] This is your HIGHEST-priority portal. Flutter operations run on Moondew Nectar, and Moondew only spawns at night (5 PM to 6 AM), so fast access pays for itself fast. You will need a steady Flutter Plort pipeline (300+ banked for the Chapter 9 Quantum Drones), and this portal is what keeps it flowing.
