[h3]Phase 1: Finding Ghostly Drones[/h3]

[b]What Are Ghostly Drones?[/b]
Ethereal, semi-transparent phantoms that drift around Rainbow Island. Interact with one and it hands over a Drone Station Module, then vanishes for good. No second chances, so do not fumble the prompt.

[b]Easiest 3 Locations (for Chapter 9):[/b]
[list]
[*][b]First Ranch Expansion:[/b] 1 Ghostly Drone in whichever expansion you unlocked first (Gully, Archway, or Den)
[*][b]The Tidepools:[/b] 1 Ghostly Drone spawn
[*][b]Starlight Strand:[/b] By the big two-colored tree (pink/blue biome junction)
[/list]

[b]How to Capture:[/b]
[olist]
[*]Approach the Ghostly Drone (within 5 meters)
[*]Press interact button
[*]Receive 1 Drone Station Module
[*]Drone disappears permanently
[/olist]

[h3]How Quantum Drones Work[/h3]

[b]Two Operating Modes:[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Rancher Mode[/b] (Placed in ranch expansions):
  - Program with Target → Source → Destination logic
  - Instantly teleports between locations
  - Handles all ranch automation
[*][b]Explorer Mode[/b] (Placed in wild biomes):
  - Generates "quantum" resources based on biome
  - Deposits into shared Quantum Cloud inventory (max 9,999 items)
  - Passive resource generation while you are away
  - [b](Patch 1.1.0)[/b] Can now target Odd Onions; nerfed to curb runaway plort farming
[/list]

[b]Battery System:[/b]
[list]
[*]Runs on plorts: 0%-100% (green bar) with overcharge to 999% (golden bar)
[*]Initial battery: 24%
[*]Higher battery % = faster operation speed
[*]Battery only drains during active task execution
[*]Picking up a station resets battery to 24%
[/list]

[b]Important Limitations:[/b]
[list]
[*]Drones only work within the same expansion (no transfers between Conservatory/Gully/etc.)
[*]Food to Auto-Feeders scatters across ALL feeders randomly (you lose the favorite-food bonus)
[*][b]Recommendation:[/b] Use drones ONLY for plort collection. Feed by hand to keep the 4-plort bonus.
[/list]
