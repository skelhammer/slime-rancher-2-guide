[h2]Introduction: The Rancher's Mindset[/h2]

You wash up on Rainbow Island with a Vacpack, a run-down shack, and a dangerous amount of optimism. Other guides will tell you to slow down and smell the Pogofruit. Ignore them.

This isn't a cozy farm sim. It's a supply chain wearing a farm sim's clothes. Every slime is a revenue line, every plot is capital tied up in real estate, and every minute you spend hand-feeding chickens is a minute the machine isn't running itself. Do it right and six Tabby slimes snowball into a Newbuck empire that prints while you're off exploring. Do it wrong and you drown in Tarr.

[b]Deploy capital aggressively. Automate relentlessly. Dominate the market.[/b]

[h2]Core Mechanics: Understanding the Fundamentals[/h2]

A handful of systems decide whether your ranch compounds or stalls. Learn these cold before you pour a single Newbuck into a plot.

[h3]📈 The Plort Market: Supply, Demand, and How to Game It[/h3]

Plort prices ride supply and demand, and the market has a short memory. Dump a hundred of one plort today and tomorrow it is worth pennies. Sell a little of everything and prices stay fat across the board.

[b]What that means for you:[/b]
[list]
[*]Spread production across several slime types so no single price ever craters
[*]Sell a balanced mix every day instead of flooding one plort and tanking it
[*]The pro move: hoard one plort type while you flood the rest, then dump the stockpile the day its price spikes
[/list]

[h3]Largo Slimes: Two Slimes' Output, One Slime's Footprint[/h3]

Largos are where the money is. Feed any slime a plort from a different species and it merges into a Largo: same corral space, twice the product line. A Largo pays out [b]both[/b] plort types every time you feed it.

[b]The Largo math:[/b]
[list]
[*]Base slime + normal food = 1 plort (~10 NB)
[*]Base slime + favorite food = 2 plorts, same type (~20 NB)
[*]Largo + normal food = 2 plorts, one of each (~30-50 NB)
[*][b]Largo + favorite food = 4 plorts total, two of each (60-150+ NB)[/b]
[/list]

A well-fed Largo quadruples the output of a lone base slime. That ratio is the entire engine, so build everything around it.

[h3]⚠️ CRITICAL WARNING: Tarr Outbreaks and Prevention[/h3]

Here is the mistake that ends runs. Feed a Largo a [b]third[/b] plort type, one it does not already make, and it does not get fatter. It turns into a [b]Tarr[/b]: a black, oily horror that devours your slimes, devours you, and turns everything it touches into more Tarr. One stray plort can chew through an entire ranch in minutes.

[b]Prevention (non-negotiable):[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Plort Collectors on every corral. No exceptions.[/b] They vacuum loose plorts off the ground before any slime gets ideas
[*]Install them before you wander off, not after. The outbreak will not wait for you
[*]A single missed plort is all it takes
[/list]

[b]If it all goes wrong anyway:[/b]
[list]
[*]Water kills Tarr on contact, so keep a full Water Tank and start spraying
[*]Move now. Seconds matter, and the Tarr count only climbs
[*]When a corral is lost, cut your losses: vacuum the whole thing into the Slime Sea
[/list]

[h3]The Agitation System: Managing Slime Behavior[/h3]

Every slime carries a hidden agitation meter. Let it climb and your tidy corrals turn into a tantrum. Keep it low and the ranch hums.

[b]How agitation builds:[/b]
[list]
[*]It starts ticking up once a slime hits 99% hunger
[*]From zero, it takes about 12 in-game hours to max out
[*]The higher it climbs, the worse everyone behaves:
  - Rock slimes roll more, and that roll hurts
  - Boom slimes detonate more often, flinging plorts everywhere
  - Crystal slimes throw out more damaging spikes
  - [b]Hunter Largos snap back to feral[/b] and turn on you
[/list]

[b]How to keep it down:[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Feeding:[/b] the only thing that fully resets agitation to zero
[*][b]Music Box[/b] (350 NB): halves how fast agitation grows
[*][b]Slime Toys:[/b] shave 12.5% off growth, or 25% for a favorite. [b]Patch 1.1.0:[/b] every slime now has a favorite toy
[*][b]Auto-Feeders[/b] (900 NB): the real answer. They drip food on a timer and keep agitation flat
[/list]

[h3]Optimal Corral Population[/h3]

Cramming a corral full backfires: overcrowding spikes agitation and actually drops output. Aim for the sweet spot in a fully upgraded corral:
[list]
[*]8-10 regular slimes
[*]5-6 Largos
[/list]

More slimes is not more money. It is just more chaos.
