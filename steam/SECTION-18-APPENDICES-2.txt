[h3]Slime Toys (Patch 1.1.0)[/h3]
[list]
[*]Every slime now has a favorite toy (25% agitation reduction)
[*]Non-favorite toys: 12.5% reduction
[*]Purchased from ProntoMart's Coo-Coo Corner (1 new toy available daily)
[*]Some toys have special "curious effects" beyond agitation reduction
[*][b]Notable toys:[/b] Big Rock (Rock), Buzzy Bee (Honey), Stego Buddy (Saber), Rocket Ship (Hyper), Stuffed Sheep (Cotton)
[*][b](Patch 1.2) Resource Detector:[/b] Toy for the rancher - pick up to track direction of nearby science/crafting resources. Major utility for material hunting.
[/list]

[h3]Radiant Slimes (Patch 1.2)[/h3]
[list]
[*]Rare prismatic-altered variants of every slime type
[*]Spawn anywhere their normal version appears (very rare)
[*]Behavior matches the base slime; ranch/largo as normal
[*]Take to The Sanctuary so the Caretaker can shed Prisma energy while keeping radiant colors
[*][b]Lost and Found[/b] in Sanctuary recovers any Radiant Slimes that reverted to base form
[/list]

[h3]The Sanctuary (Patch 1.2)[/h3]
[list]
[*]New ranch expansion accessed through the Grey Labyrinth
[*]Run by the Caretaker NPC
[*]Themed, decoratable rooms with alcoves for favored slimes
[*][b]Sprinkles (new currency):[/b] Generated by decorating Sanctuary rooms - spent to unlock more rooms, themes, gadgets, and decor
[*]Customization / collection sink - not a plort production expansion
[/list]

[h3]Plot Upgrades (Patch 1.2)[/h3]
[list]
[*][b]Silo - Improved Storage Capacity:[/b] Doubles per-slot capacity 100 -> 200. Stacks with all Tier upgrades. Fully upgraded silo holds 2,400 items. [b]Highest-leverage Silo upgrade in the game - install before scaling production.[/b]
[*][b]Garden - Growth Amplifier:[/b] Expands growing space per garden plot for higher crop output. Stacks with Nutrient Soil + Sprinkler.
[/list]

[h3]Gadget Bundles (Patch 1.2)[/h3]
[list]
[*]ProntoMart's Coo-Coo Corner now sells 22 themed multi-item bundles
[*]Themes include: Rainbow Fields, Tide Pools, Ember Valley, Starlight Strand, Powderfall Bluffs, Labyrinth, Conservatory, Map Markers, Engineer Starter, Music, Lights, Beach Day, Planter, Bait, Magma, Cave, Wind, Scientist, Reefs, Grass, Flowers, Fences and Walls
[*]Use bundles for cost-efficient theme building in Sanctuary rooms or biome-matched Conservatory zones
[/list]
