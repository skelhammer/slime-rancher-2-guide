[h1]Slime Combinations, Upgrades & Tips[/h1]

[h2]Essential Slime Combinations[/h2]

[h3]Early Game Largos (Chapters 1-4)[/h3]
[list]
[*][b]Tabby-Cotton[/b]: Easy to maintain, Water Lettuce is abundant (Cotton favorite)
[*][b]Phosphor-Honey[/b]: Crafting plorts, feed Cuberries (Phosphor favorite)
[*][b]Crystal-Rock[/b]: High value, feed Heart Beets (Rock favorite)
[*][b]Boom-Batty[/b]: Both nocturnal, feed Pomegranites (Batty favorite)
[/list]

[h3]Mid-Game Largos (Chapters 5-8)[/h3]
[list]
[*][b]Angler-Crystal[/b]: Sea Hen meat eaters (Angler favorite)
[*][b]Saber-Hunter[/b]: Elite meat combo, feed Thunderclucks (Saber favorite)
[*][b]Flutter-Honey[/b]: Nectar eaters, calming aura, requires Solar Shield
[/list]

[h3]Advanced Largos (Chapters 10+)[/h3]
[list]
[*][b]Dervish-Ringtail[/b]: Weather event slimes, Prickle Pears (Dervish favorite)
[*][b]Boom-Tangle[/b]: Meat + weather slimes, Painted Hens (Tangle favorite)
[*][b]Tabby-Saber[/b]: High-value Tabby combo (Tabby favorite: Stony Hens)
[/list]

[*][b]Dash Boots II[/b] (1,800 NB + materials) - Chapter 4+

[h3]Ranch Infrastructure[/h3]
[olist]

[*][b]Auto-Feeders[/b] (900 NB each) - Essential for automation
[*][b]High Walls[/b] (260 NB) - Required for Cotton, Ringtail, and jumping slimes
[*][b]Solar Shields[/b] (425 NB) - Required for Phosphor, Batty, and all nocturnal Largos

[h2]Critical Tips[/h2]

[h3]Ranch Management[/h3]
[list]
[*][b]Never exceed 6 Largos per corral[/b] - Overcrowding = agitation
[*][b]Always use favorite foods[/b] - 4 plorts vs 2 plorts per feeding
[*][b]Stockpile Flutter Plorts early[/b] - Need 300+ for Quantum Drones (Chapter 9)
[*][b]Install Auto-Feeders before long trips[/b] - Prevents agitation during exploration
[/list]

[h3]Resource Farming[/h3]
[list]
[*][b]Jellystone[/b] - Ember Valley caves, craft early upgrades
[*][b]Deep Brine[/b] - Ember Valley pools, needed for all advanced upgrades
[*][b]Radiant Ore[/b] - Ember Valley glowing deposits
[*][b]Buzz Wax & Wild Honey[/b] - Starlight Strand, critical for Tier III upgrades
[*][b]Sun Sap & Perfect Snowflakes[/b] - Powderfall Bluffs, endgame materials
[/list]

[h3]Market Strategy[/h3]
[list]
[*][b]Diversify daily sales[/b] - Sell 3-5 different plort types per day
[*][b]Avoid market flooding[/b] - Don't dump 100+ of one plort type
[*][b]Watch for price spikes[/b] - Some days offer 150-200% normal prices
[*][b]Hoard high-value plorts[/b] - Save Saber, Hyper, Shadow plorts for spike days
[/list]

[h3]Exploration[/h3]
[list]
[*][b]Pop Gordos strategically[/b] - They unlock portals, paths, and treasure pods
[*][b]Collect Map Data Nodes[/b] - Reveals area layout and resource locations
[*][b]Hunt Treasure Pods early[/b] - Many contain blueprint upgrades
[*][b]Ghostly Drones unlock automation[/b] - Find 3 for Quantum Drone Stations
[/list]
