[h3]Understanding the Threat[/h3]

[b]What is the Prismacore?[/b]
An extraterrestrial object that crashed into Rainbow Island, the source of all Prisma Disruption in the Grey Labyrinth.

[b]The Escalating Crisis:[/b]
[list]
[*]Areas around the Prismacore are slowly turning to gold, structures failing and vanishing
[*]Left unchecked: Dream Land falls, the Labyrinth atmosphere collapses, Prismacore energy radiates across the Far, Far Range
[*]All life (slimes included) is lost
[/list]

[b]Your Mission:[/b] Stabilize the Prismacore permanently. No partial measures.

[b]Location:[/b] Heart of the Grey Labyrinth (accessed through Impossible Sky)

[b]Access Requirements:[/b]

[h3]Phase 4: Activate the 5 Harmonizers[/h3]

[b]Location:[/b] Hidden throughout the Grey Labyrinth zones

[b]Activation:[/b] Shoot any item at each Harmonizer to switch it on. Fruits, vegetables, plorts, chickens, anything works.

[b]Harmonizer Locations:[/b]
Distributed across the Grey Labyrinth zones. Sweep each area systematically:
[list]
[*]Prismacore Surroundings - check the gilded areas near the core
[*]Dream Land zones (East, West, Central) - look for elevated platforms and hidden alcoves
[*]Impossible Sky zones - explore vertical structures and floating platforms
[*]The Mirage - search the desert-like areas with unstable terrain
[*]Shifting Caverns - check the cave systems and tunnels
[/list]

[b]Search Strategy:[/b]
[list]
[*]Harmonizers emit a faint glow and a distinctive sound when you're close
[*]Use your map to track which zones you've cleared
[*]Some require platforming or navigating around Stabilizers
[/list]

[b]Status Tracking:[/b] The game counts your activated Harmonizers. All 5 must be live before you can stabilize the Prismacore. No shortcuts.

[hr][/hr]

[h3]Phase 5: Prismacore Stabilization[/h3]

[b]⚠️ The Tarr Barrier:[/b]
[list]
[*]A giant Tarr barrier surrounds the Prismacore's core
[*]It cannot be crossed or destroyed with water
[/list]

[b]The Slime Shower:[/b]
A large facility inside the Prismacore built to break the Tarr barrier. It runs on slime sacrifice.
[list]
[*][b]Two depositing ports:[/b] pick either one (both work identically)
[*][b]Requirement:[/b] deposit 6 slimes total into a single port
[*][b]Any slime type works:[/b] Pink, Rock, Phosphor, Largos, it doesn't matter
[*][b]Source options:[/b]
  - Bring 6 slimes from your ranch (vacpack them up before entering)
  - Catch 6 wild slimes in the Grey Labyrinth zones
  - A mix of both
[/list]

[b]Activation Process:[/b]
[olist]
[*]Approach either depositing port
[*]Shoot 6 slimes into the port (they get absorbed)
[*]The Slime Shower fires automatically
[*]The Tarr barrier dissolves, opening the path to the Prismacore core
[/olist]

[b]Strategic Note:[/b] Wild Labyrinth slimes are the smart sacrifice here. Don't feed your high-value ranch Largos into the machine when free Pink slimes do the exact same job.

[b]Nullifier Deployment:[/b]
[list]
[*]With the Tarr barrier down, approach the Prismacore core
[*]Deploy the Nullifier device
[*][b]Cutscene:[/b] Rainbow Island stabilizes, the Prisma Disruptions cease
[/list]
