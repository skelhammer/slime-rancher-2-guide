[h1]Part 2: The Industrial Revolution[/h1]

[h2]Chapter 6: The Tidepools Passive Income[/h2]

[b]Primary Objective:[/b] Stand up a fully passive income operation that runs itself with minimal daily babysitting.

[b]Mindset:[/b] This is the chapter where your ranch starts printing money while you sleep. Puddle and Fire Slimes are the closest thing to a savings account this island offers: low touch, zero Tarr risk, steady yield. Build it once, skim the profits forever.

[hr][/hr]

[h3]Financial Checkpoint[/h3]

[list]
[*][b]Starting Budget:[/b] 25,000-30,000+ NB from Chapter 5
[*][b]Capital Required:[/b] ~24,350 NB total
  - Tidepools unlock: 6,000 NB
  - Build-out: 4,350 NB
  - Tier III upgrades: 14,000 NB
[*][b]Revenue Impact:[/b] ~5,540-6,810 NB/day (up from ~4,500)
[*][b]Passive Income:[/b] +1,540-2,310 NB/day for only 10-15 min of management
[*][b]Timeline:[/b] 5-7 days through all operations
[/list]

[hr][/hr]

[h3]Phase 1: The Tidepools Build (5 Plots)[/h3]

This is the lowest-effort revenue on the island. Set it and forget it.

[b]Plot 1 & 2 - Puddle Ponds:[/b]
[list]
[*]Pond (450 NB each) × 2 = 900 NB
[*]4 Puddle Slimes per pond (8 total)
[*]Zero-maintenance income: they generate plorts straight from water
[/list]

[b]Plot 3 & 4 - Fire Incinerators:[/b]
[list]
[*]Incinerator + Ash Trough (950 NB each) × 2 = 1,900 NB
[*]5 Fire Slimes per incinerator (10 total)
[*]Low-maintenance income: feed ash made from vegetables
[/list]

[b]Plot 5 - Ash Production Garden:[/b]
[list]
[*]Garden + Nutrient Soil + Sprinkler = 1,550 NB
[*]Plant Odd Onions (or any veggie)
[*]Toss vegetables into the incinerators to generate ash
[/list]

[b]Total Investment:[/b] 6,000 NB (unlock) + 4,350 NB (build) = 10,350 NB

[hr][/hr]

[h3]Phase 2: Tier III Upgrades (MANDATORY for Chapter 7)[/h3]

[b]⚠️ CRITICAL - Required Before Powderfall Bluffs:[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Tank Booster III[/b] (3,000 NB + materials): 40 to 50 units per slot
[*][b]Power Core III[/b] (3,000 NB + materials): 200 to 250 Energy
[*][b]Heart Module III[/b] (3,000 NB + materials): 200 to 250 HP
[*][b]Tank Guard II[/b] (5,000 NB + materials): salvages 40% on damage
[/list]

[b]Total Investment:[/b] ~14,000 NB + materials

[b]Why MANDATORY:[/b] Chapter 7 (Powderfall Bluffs) is crawling with aggressive Saber Slimes. Walk in without maxed defenses and you will be making the trip back to the ranch a lot.

[hr][/hr]

[h3]Strategic Priorities[/h3]

[olist]
[*][b]Unlock the Tidepools:[/b] buy the expansion (6,000 NB) from the far end of the Gully
[*][b]Deploy Puddle Ponds:[/b] 8 slimes total for zero-maintenance plort generation
[*][b]Deploy Fire Incinerators:[/b] 10 Fire Slimes for low-maintenance income
[*][b]Build the Ash Garden:[/b] plant vegetables to feed the Fire Slimes
[*][b]Craft Tier III Upgrades:[/b] non-negotiable for surviving Chapter 7 Powderfall Bluffs
[*][b]Establish Passive Income:[/b] 1,540-2,310 NB/day for just 10-15 min of management
[*][b]Zero Tarr Risk:[/b] Puddle and Fire Slimes cannot create Tarr, so this operation never bites back
[/olist]
