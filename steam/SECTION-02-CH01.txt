[h1]Part 1: The Foundation[/h1]

[h2]Chapter 1: The Sprint to Mobility[/h2]

[b]Primary Objective:[/b] Craft the Jetpack and unlock vertical exploration.

[b]Mindset:[/b] Everything is mobility. Nothing else matters yet. This is a 72-hour blitz with one non-negotiable goal: the Jetpack. It is the key to the rest of the island, so until it is strapped to your back, every Newbuck and every minute funnels toward it. No detours, no decorating.

[hr][/hr]

[h3]Financial Checkpoint[/h3]

[list]
[*][b]Starting Budget:[/b] 0 Newbucks (fresh off the boat)
[*][b]Capital Required:[/b] ~2,535 NB total
  - Ranch Setup: 1,185 NB (Plot 1 Corral + Plot 2 Garden + upgrades)
  - Vacpack Upgrades: 1,350 NB (Resource Harvester, Heart Module, Jetpack)
[*][b]Daily Revenue:[/b] 300-500 NB/day from Tabby-Cotton Largos
[*][b]Timeline:[/b] 5-7 days to bank the funds
[/list]

[hr][/hr]

[h3]Phase 1: Initial Asset Collection[/h3]

Your first workforce is cheap, common, and already waiting in the grass.

[b]Slimes Needed:[/b]
[list]
[*]6 Tabby Slimes (throughout Rainbow Fields)
[*]6 Cotton Plorts (from wild Cotton Slimes in grassy areas)
[/list]

[b]Food Stockpile:[/b]
[list]
[*]20+ Water Lettuce (wild patches near water)
[/list]

[b]Collection Strategy:[/b]
[olist]
[*]Capture 6 Tabby Slimes first
[*]Collect 6 Cotton Plorts from wild Cotton Slimes
[*]Feed Cotton Plorts to Tabby Slimes to create Tabby-Cotton Largos
[*]Harvest Water Lettuce and stockpile it for feeding
[/olist]

[hr][/hr]

[h3]Phase 2: Plot-by-Plot Build[/h3]

[b]Plot 1 - Tabby-Cotton Largos:[/b]
[list]
[*]Build Corral (250 NB)
[*]Add High Walls (260 NB): stops jumping escapes
[*]Add Air Net (425 NB): keeps plorts from flying out
[*][b]Total: 935 NB[/b]
[*]Purpose: your primary revenue stream
[*]Feed Water Lettuce (Cotton favorite) for 4 plorts per feeding
[/list]

[b]Plot 2 - Water Lettuce Garden:[/b]
[list]
[*]Build Garden (250 NB)
[*]Plant Water Lettuce
[*]No upgrades yet (the Jetpack comes first)
[*]Nutrient Soil + Sprinkler get added in Chapter 2
[/list]

[b]Plots 3-8:[/b]
[list]
[*]Leave them empty. This is a strategic hold, not laziness.
[*]Every spare Newbuck is reserved for the Jetpack
[/list]

[b]Total Investment:[/b] 1,185 NB (plots + upgrades)

[hr][/hr]

[h3]Phase 3: Unlock Ember Valley[/h3]

[b]Pink Gordo:[/b]
[list]
[*]Location: Rainbow Fields, western area near the slime statues
[*]Feed 30 Any Food (it is an omnivore and eats anything)
[*]Reward: Geyser to the Ember Valley portal switch, 12 Pink Slimes, and 1 jar
[/list]

[b]Why Ember Valley?[/b]
[list]
[*]Radiant Ore spawns abundantly here, and the Jetpack needs it
[*]Opens access to resources you will lean on for chapters to come
[/list]

[hr][/hr]

[h3]Phase 4: Resource Gathering for Jetpack[/h3]

[b]Night Hunt - Phosphor Slimes:[/b]
[list]
[*]Wait for nightfall in Rainbow Fields
[*]Hunt Phosphor Slimes (they spawn near the central mountain and western areas)
[*]Collect 10 Phosphor Plorts
[*]Do not ranch them yet (that needs a Solar Shield, 425 NB)
[*]Get out before dawn. Sunlight burns them.
[/list]

[b]Ember Valley - Radiant Ore:[/b]
[list]
[*]Enter Ember Valley through the portal (after popping the Pink Gordo)
[*]Harvest 10 Radiant Ore from the glowing pink/magenta crystal nodes
[*][b]⚠️ CRITICAL:[/b] Craft the Resource Harvester first (450 NB + 10 Cotton Plorts)
[/list]

[hr][/hr]

[h3]Phase 5: Vacpack Upgrades[/h3]

[b]Crafting Order:[/b]
[olist]
[*][b]Resource Harvester[/b] (450 NB + 10 Cotton Plorts)
  - Lets you harvest science resources from nodes
  - [b]CRAFT FIRST:[/b] required before you can touch Radiant Ore
[*][b]Heart Module[/b] (450 NB + 10 Pink Plorts)
  - Raises HP from 100 to 150
  - Your survival cushion for Ember Valley
[*][b]Jetpack[/b] (450 NB + 10 Phosphor Plorts + 10 Radiant Ore)
  - Unlocks the personal jetpack
  - [b]THE PRIZE:[/b] vertical exploration is finally yours
[/olist]

[b]Total Investment:[/b] 1,350 NB (upgrades)

[hr][/hr]

[h3]Phase 6: Core Mechanics Reference[/h3]

[b]Creating Largos:[/b]
[list]
[*]Feed a slime a different plort type to convert it
[*]Example: Tabby Slime + Cotton Plort = Tabby-Cotton Largo
[*]Largos produce 2 plort types when fed
[*]A Largo fed its favorite food = 4 plorts total (2 of each type)
[/list]

[b]Refinery vs Plort Market:[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Refinery[/b] (in the Lab): deposits plorts for crafting. [b]No withdrawals[/b], so spend deliberately
[*][b]Plort Market:[/b] sells plorts for cold Newbucks
[/list]

[b]Key Details:[/b]
[list]
[*]Optimal corral population: 6 Largos (or 8-10 regular slimes)
[*]Water Lettuce is the Cotton favorite (4 plorts per feeding)
[/list]
