[h1]Part 3: Automation & Empire[/h1]

[h2]Chapter 11: The Archway Grey Labyrinth Operations[/h2]

[b]Primary Objective:[/b] Fold the Grey Labyrinth slimes into your automated ranch and stand up the highest-value plort line in the game.

[b]Mindset:[/b] This is the chapter where the ranch stops being a farm and becomes a printing press. The Grey Labyrinth elites are premium assets. Treat them that way: feed them their favorites, lock them down, and let the automation collect the dividends while you are off exploring.

[h3]Phase 1: The Archway Build (5 Plots)[/h3]

[b]Plot 1 - Polaricherry Garden:[/b]
[list]
[*]Garden + Nutrient Soil + Sprinkler = 1,550 NB
[*]Feeds Plot 2
[*]Polaricherry from Grey Labyrinth (20 per harvest)
[/list]

[b]Plot 2 - Twin-Flutter Largos:[/b]
[list]
[*]Corral + High Walls + Auto-Feeder + Plort Collector = 2,010 NB
[*]Feed Polaricherry (Twin favorite) for 4 plorts per feeding
[*]Revenue: ~100-150 NB/feeding
[*]Flutter brings a calming aura, Twin portals grab distant food. Low-maintenance money.
[/list]

[b]Plot 3 - Candied Hen Coop:[/b]
[list]
[*]Coop + High Walls + Spring Grass + Vitamizer = 1,750 NB
[*]Stock: 6-8 hens + 2 roostros
[*]Feeds Plot 4
[/list]

[b]Plot 4 - Sloomber-Saber Largos:[/b]
[list]
[*]Corral + High Walls + Music Box + Auto-Feeder + Plort Collector = 2,360 NB
[*]Feed Candied Hens (Sloomber favorite) for 4 plorts per feeding
[*]Revenue: ~150-250 NB/feeding
[*][b]HIGHEST VALUE COMBO IN THE GAME[/b]
[*][b]⚠️ CRITICAL:[/b] Music Box is mandatory. Sloomber sleep clouds are the only thing keeping the Saber half docile.
[/list]

[b]Plot 5 - Silo:[/b]
[list]
[*]Silo + Tier 1 + Tier 2 + Tier 3 = 2,175 NB
[*]Store Twin, Sloomber, Saber, Flutter plorts plus a food buffer
[*][b]⚠️ Do not[/b] dump Grey Labyrinth plorts into the Refinery yet. You need them in Chapters 13-14.
[/list]

[b]Total Investment:[/b] 2,800 NB (unlock) + 9,845 NB (build) = 12,645 NB
