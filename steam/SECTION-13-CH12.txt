[h3]Phase 1: The Digsite Build (4 Plots) - OPTIONAL[/h3]

Two premium operations, two food supply lines. This is a niche wing of the empire, but the margins are excellent.

[b]Plot 1 - Prickle Pear Garden:[/b]
[list]
[*]Garden + Nutrient Soil + Sprinkler = 1,550 NB
[*]Feeds Plot 2 (Prickle Pear is the Dervish favorite)
[/list]

[b]Plot 2 - Dervish-Ringtail Largos:[/b]
[list]
[*]Corral + High Walls + Music Box + Auto-Feeder + Plort Collector = 2,360 NB
[*]Feed Prickle Pears (Dervish favorite) for 4 plorts per feeding
[*]Revenue: ~150-200 NB/feeding
[*][b]VERY HIGH VALUE COMBO[/b]
[*]Dervish spins up tornadoes (Music Box settles them); Ringtail turns to stone in daylight
[/list]

[b]Plot 3 - Painted Hen Coop:[/b]
[list]
[*]Coop + High Walls + Spring Grass + Vitamizer = 1,750 NB
[*]Stock: 6-8 hens + 2 roostros (from Level 3 Pollen Storms)
[*]Feeds Plot 4
[/list]

[b]Plot 4 - Tangle-Hyper Largos:[/b]
[list]
[*]Corral + High Walls + Air Net + Music Box + Auto-Feeder + Plort Collector = 2,785 NB
[*]Feed Painted Hens (Tangle favorite) for 4 plorts per feeding
[*]Revenue: ~120-200 NB/feeding
[*][b]⚠️ MAXIMUM SECURITY REQUIRED:[/b] Air Net is CRITICAL (Hyper is absurdly bouncy)
[*]Tangle vines snatch food from 10 units away
[/list]

[b]Total Investment:[/b] 6,000 NB (unlock) + 8,445 NB (build) = 14,445 NB
