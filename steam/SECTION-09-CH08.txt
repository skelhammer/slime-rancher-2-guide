[h3]Pairing Rationale: Why Saber-Hunter (Not a Flyer)[/h3]

The obvious question: [i]"Shouldn't Saber pair with a flying slime?"[/i] [b]No.[/b] Saber already jumps. Bolt it to a Flutter or Batty and the largo clears walls almost instantly, Air Nets be damned. Saber-Hunter keeps both halves ground-bound. For a second Saber operation later, reach for another ground carnivore (Sloomber in Chapter 11 is the top pick), never a flyer.

[h3]Optional: Meat-Minimization Strategy[/h3]

Want to skip chicken farming entirely? Pair carnivores with non-carnivores (Hunter-Honey, Hunter-Cotton, Angler-Phosphor). The largo accepts either diet, and the fruit or veggie favorite alone triggers the 4-plort bonus.

[b]Trade-off:[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Give up:[/b] the top-tier meat combos (Saber-Hunter, Sloomber-Saber, Angler-Crystal). Premium chicken foods drive the revenue ceiling.
[*][b]Gain:[/b] no coops, no Elder Collectors, simpler drone automation, fewer moving parts. Conservatory and Gully slot counts open back up.
[/list]

[b]Choose meat-minimized[/b] for simplicity and casual play. [b]Stick with the default[/b] (Saber-Hunter + Angler-Crystal) if you are optimizing peak Newbucks/day.
