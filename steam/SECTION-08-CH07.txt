[h1]Part 2: The Industrial Revolution[/h1]

[h2]Chapter 7: Powderfall Bluffs Saber Acquisition[/h2]

[b]Primary Objective:[/b] Capture Saber Slimes and establish the resource pipeline that funds your late-game upgrades.

[b]Mindset:[/b] Powderfall is a supply run, not a sightseeing tour. You came for Sabers and for Sun Sap, the rarest material on the island. Mark every tree, grab every pod, and get out. The endgame is built here whether you notice it or not.

[h3]Phase 1: Accessing Powderfall Bluffs[/h3]

[b]Entry Location:[/b] Hidden waterfall entrance in southwestern Ember Valley

[b]How to Access:[/b]
[olist]
[*]Enter Ember Valley from Rainbow Fields
[*]Travel to the western volcanic region
[*]Look for the large waterfall near the coastal area
[*]The entrance is a frozen cave BEHIND the waterfall
[*]You will need the Jetpack to scale the vertical ice walls
[/olist]

[b]Recommended Equipment:[/b] Heart Module III (250 HP), Power Core III (250 Energy), Jetpack II, Water Tank, Pulse Wave, Tank Guard II

[hr][/hr]

[h3]Phase 2: Saber Gordo & Asset Acquisition[/h3]

[b]Saber Gordo:[/b]
[list]
[*]Location: Northern area, high mountain plateau
[*]Feed 50 Meat (Thunderclucks count 2x, so 25 birds does it)
[*]Reward: Portal shortcut to southern Ember Valley, 12 Saber Slimes, and 1 jar
[/list]

[b]Capture Strategy:[/b]
[list]
[*]10-12 Saber Slimes (pop the Saber Gordo for 12 free)
[*]20-30 Thunderclucks (including 2 Roostros for breeding)
[*]Lead with Pulse Wave for crowd control. Sabers are aggressive and fast, and they will swarm you.
[*]Haul them home via Saber Gordo portal to Ember Valley to Rainbow Fields to Conservatory
[/list]

[hr][/hr]

[h3]Phase 3: Critical Resource Extraction[/h3]

This is the real reason you came. The Sabers are a bonus.

[b]Priority Resources:[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Sun Sap[/b] (ULTRA-RARE): golden sap from frozen trees
  - The single biggest bottleneck for endgame upgrades
  - MARK EVERY TREE LOCATION for weekly farming (5-7 day respawns)
  - Target: 5-10 Sun Sap stockpiled
[*][b]Perfect Snowflakes[/b]: glimmering cliff faces. Target: 50+
[*][b]Slime Fossils[/b]: cave walls. Target: 30+
[/list]

[b]Priority Treasure Pod:[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Sun Sap x3[/b]: top of a cliff in the forested area (Jetpack required)
  - A full 30% of your total endgame Sun Sap in one pod
  - Grab it immediately. Do not leave Powderfall without it.
[/list]

[hr][/hr]

[h3]Phase 4: Upgrades & Warp Infrastructure[/h3]

[b]Craft This Upgrade:[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Extra Tank II[/b] (3,000 NB + materials): adds a 5th vac slot
  - RECOMMENDED for Powderfall resource runs, where every slot counts
[/list]

[b]Deploy Warp System:[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Refinery Link[/b] (250 NB): central valley plateau near the Map Data Node
[*][b]Powderfall Portal[/b] (2,000 NB): one-way to Powderfall (place in Conservatory)
[*][b]Blue Home Portal[/b] (500 NB): one-way to Conservatory (place in central valley)
[/list]

[b]Strategic Rationale:[/b] Sun Sap is the rarest crafting material in the game, and it respawns at a glacial 5-7 days. The portal pays for itself by turning a long expedition into a quick weekly loop past every marked tree. Build the infrastructure now and the farming runs forever.
