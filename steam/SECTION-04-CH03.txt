[h2]Chapter 3: Ember Valley Resource Extraction[/h2]

[b]Primary Objective:[/b] Expand the Conservatory to 8 plots while unlocking Tier II Vacpack upgrades.

[b]Mindset:[/b] Chapter 2 built the engine. This chapter triples its output. You are going from a tidy little farm to a real extraction operation, so think bigger: more corrals, more revenue, and the infrastructure to stop wasting your own time walking.

[h3]Phase 1: Gordo Strategy[/h3]

Gordos are free payloads of slimes, treasure, and shortcuts. Pop these five and Ember Valley opens up.

[b]Pop These 5 Ember Valley Gordos:[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Rock Gordo[/b] (50 Veggies) → Jetpack Drive treasure pod + 12 Rock Slimes
[*][b]Batty Gordo[/b] (50 Fruit) → Tunnel shortcut + 12 Batty Slimes + Power Core II Blueprint + slime science resource jar
[*][b]Crystal Gordo[/b] (50 Veggies) → Geyser + 12 Crystal Slimes
[*][b]Boom Gordo[/b] (50 Meat) → Grey Labyrinth gate + Strange Diamond + Ember Valley Portal + 12 Boom Slimes
[*][b]Tabby Gordo[/b] (50 Meat) → Portal to Rainbow Fields + 12 Tabby Slimes
[/list]

[hr][/hr]

[h3]Phase 2: Asset Acquisition[/h3]

Two new largo lines go on the books here. Round up the raw stock first.

[b]Slimes Needed:[/b]
[list]
[*]6 Crystal Slimes (northern area near the chasm)
[*]6 Boom Slimes (volcanic areas throughout)
[*]6 Rock Plorts (feed to Crystal Slimes for Crystal-Rock Largos)
[*]6 Batty Plorts (feed to Boom Slimes for Boom-Batty Largos)
[/list]

[b]Food Stockpile:[/b]
[list]
[*]30+ Heart Beets (Rock favorite)
[*]30+ Pomegranites (Batty favorite)
[/list]

[b]Alternate Strategy:[/b] Pop the Crystal and Boom Gordos for 12 free slimes of each type and skip the wild capture entirely.

[h3]Phase 4: Tier II Upgrades[/h3]

[b]Craft Immediately:[/b]
[list]
[*][b]⚠️ Water Tank[/b] (450 NB + 10 Deep Brine): CRAFT FIRST. Your essential Tarr safety net
[*][b]Dash Boots[/b] (450 NB + 10 Boom Plort): reduced sprint energy cost
[*][b]Extra Tank[/b] (1,500 NB + 10 Rock Plort + 10 Silky Sand): adds a 4th vac slot
[*][b]Power Core II[/b] (1,000 NB + materials + Power Chip): raises Energy from 150 to 200
[/list]

[b]Total Investment:[/b] ~3,400 NB + materials

[b]Required Components:[/b]
[list]
[*]Jetpack Drive (Rock Gordo treasure pod)
[*]Power Chip (Rainbow Fields treasure pod: cave near the Starlight Strand portal, among the glowing mushrooms; the Batty Gordo gives only the Power Core II BLUEPRINT, not the Power Chip component)
[*]Heart Cell (underground ledge before the Crystal Gordo)
[*]Storage Cell (behind the Rainbow Fields portal, 1 Batty Plort statue)
[/list]

[h3]Phase 5: Warp Infrastructure[/h3]

Walking is overhead. This is the upgrade that pays you back in minutes.

[b]Deploy in Ember Valley:[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Refinery Link[/b] (250 NB + materials): central geyser area between the Batty/Crystal Gordo caves
[*][b]Ember Valley Portal[/b] (2,000 NB + materials): one-way to Ember Valley (place in the Conservatory)
[*][b]Red Home Portal[/b] (1,000 NB + materials): one-way to the Conservatory (place in the Ember Valley central area)
[/list]

[b]Total Investment:[/b] 3,250 NB + materials

[b]Strategic Rationale:[/b] Cuts 5-10 minutes off every resource run. Once you are farming materials daily, that time compounds fast.
