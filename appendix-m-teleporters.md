# Appendix M: Teleporter Network

All teleporters across Rainbow Island and the Grey Labyrinth, how they pair, and how each end is activated.

Teleporters come in linked pairs - each end must be activated separately, usually by finding the nearby **Teleporter Activator** or by bursting that zone's Gordo. Once both ends are active you can travel either direction.

| Teleporter Link | How to Activate |
| --- | --- |
| Rainbow Fields &harr; Starlight Strand | Teleporter Activator at each end; the Starlight Strand end can also be activated by bursting the **Ringtail Gordo**. |
| Rainbow Fields &harr; Ember Valley | Teleporter Activator at each end; the Ember Valley end can also be activated by bursting the **Tabby Gordo**. |
| Ember Valley &harr; Powderfall Bluffs | The Ember Valley end auto-activates past the Boom/Tabby/Batty Plort Door; the Powderfall end auto-activates after the first trip and can also be activated by bursting the **Saber Gordo**. |
| Ember Valley &harr; The Grey Labyrinth | The Grey Labyrinth Teleporter. The link is opened from the Starlight Strand cave behind the **Flutter Gordo** by placing two Radiant Projectors to realign the beam (see the Ember Valley "Slime Gate" in [Appendix I](appendix-i-doors.md)). |
| The Grey Labyrinth &harr; Rainbow Fields | Activated by bursting the **Twin Gordo**. |
| Terrarium Teleporter &rarr; The Terrarium | Activated by bursting the **Sloomber Gordo**. |
| Dream Land Teleporter &rarr; Dream Land | A Grey Labyrinth internal teleporter. |

> **Note on the Sloomber Gordo:** MapGenie's teleporter list shows the Sloomber Gordo activating the **Terrarium** Teleporter (matching this guide), while MapGenie's *Gordo* page labels the same unlock a "Conservatory Teleporter." The two MapGenie pages disagree; this guide follows the teleporter listing. Verify in-game if it affects your route.
